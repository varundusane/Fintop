Home Loan

First Home Buyers

The initial footsteps to buying a home are always the most challenging. At FinTop we enjoy the opportunity to help a first home buyer with their property purchase. It is an exciting but often nerve wracking time for you so it helps to have someone there to bounce ideas off and get guidance from.

FinTop Team will:

  • Work with you to determine your needs.
  • Take you through the process of obtaining pre-approval and outline the steps involved in purchasing a property.
  • Handle all the paperwork and keep you fully informed at every stage.
  • Be on hand to assist with tips on negotiating with agents, buying at auction and, in consultation with your legal representative, help you with the contracts that need to be signed.
  • Manage deadlines including valuations, finance dates and settlement.
  • Free property reports.

Moving Home Mortgages

FinTop Team can help you be thoroughly prepared to move home by arranging a pre-approval first so you can sell and buy again with confidence. We can help clients through the process of selling and buying again.

  • Determine the new loan amount you will need including agents fees and paying out your old mortgage
  • Help you select the most competitive, suitable products from our extensive panel of lenders
  • Take you through bridging finance if you choose to buy before you sell
  • Handle all the paperwork and keep you fully informed
  • Manage deadlines including valuations and settlement
  • Review your home loan regularly and as often as you wish to check it is still competitive and that it continues to meet your needs
